## Lane A: CI standards + coverage floor (branch `wave2/ci-standards`)

**Task:** enforce the code-quality standards deterministically in CI and
close the known coverage gaps so the floor starts high.

**Approach:** add `pyproject.toml` (ruff/mypy/coverage config) and
`requirements-dev.txt` (pinned, 7-day-cooldown-checked). Extend `tests`
job with `pytest --cov --cov-fail-under=<measured floor>`. New jobs:
ruff check + format-check, mypy, gitleaks (SHA-pinned action), pip-audit
over requirements-dev.txt, lychee link check (SHA-pinned, offline-mode
for repo-relative links plus external with retry). Close the measured
coverage gaps: tests for the three unexercised validator branches
(findings-not-a-list, finding-missing-fields, non-bool validated), the
error diagnostics (_repo_root outside a repo, merge-base
unrelated-histories, git-diff failure), and one subprocess invocation of
`main()`/`--hash-only`/missing-`--pr`. Target ~95%+, floor set just under
the measured result.
